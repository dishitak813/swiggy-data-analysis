# Swiggy Restaurant Data Analysis

## Project Objective
To analyse Swiggy restaurant data and answer key business questions related to city-wise distribution, pricing trends, and customer rating behaviour using Python.

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Plotly
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## Key Business Questions
1. Which cities have the highest number of restaurants?
2. How are restaurant ratings distributed?
3. Is there a relationship between pricing and customer ratings?
4. Do ratings vary significantly across cities?

---

## Key Insights
- Metropolitan cities dominate Swiggy’s restaurant listings.
- Most restaurants maintain ratings above 3.5.
- Pricing shows a weak correlation with customer ratings.
- Customer satisfaction appears to depend more on quality than cost.

---

## Dataset
Contains 8,680 restaurant records with 10 features, including:
- City
- Area
- Price
- Average rating
- Total ratings
- Food type
- Delivery time
